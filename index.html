Simulation

Motivation

With simulation, we can study:

  • systems that do not exist (yet)
  • systems that do exist, but without disrupting them
  • different layouts and configurations
  • and behavior over a long period of time
What is simulation?

experimentation with a model

What is a model?

simplified abstraction of reality

which embraces only the scope and level of detail needed to satisfy specific study objectives
